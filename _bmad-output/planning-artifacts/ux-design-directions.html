space - Design Direction Showcase

8 directions derived from tactical tone, medium-dense layout, fly-out drilldowns, and confidence-first turn submission.

Evaluation Lens

  • Does the direction keep campaign context visible while drilling down?
  • Can a player identify critical actions in one pass?
  • Are warning and critical signals obvious but not noisy?
  • Does it avoid low-actionability information overload?
  • Does it preserve desktop depth + mobile quick-turn continuity?
